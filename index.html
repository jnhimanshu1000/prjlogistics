Company Profile

PRJ Logistics Ltd. was started in 1995 with one objective, to professionalize the transportation industry. All our hubs and warehouses are computerized. Logistics, as a business concept, evolved greatly after World War II due to the increasing complexities of supply and transportation management. The dynamics of today's economic scenario has made companies to focus their energy and resources on the safety transportation of their goods without any hindrances. Catering to this immediate need, PRJ Logistics Ltd. was established in the year 1995.

Upcoming & Diversified Business

Company uses its extensive domain knowledge of transportation business obtained through working with leading operators in roadways to convert its business into state of art solution. As the dominant professional player in the transportation industry, PRJ Logistics Ltd. uses technology that surpasses the competition with superior services spread across the widest network and has ears on the ground to connect with its loyal and ever expanding customer base.
