Company Profile

Chartered Logistics Ltd. was started on 16th June 1995 with one objective, to professionalize the transportation industry in India. Our 100 percent branches are computerized. Logistics, as a business concept, evolved greatly after World War II due to the increasing complexities of supply and transportation management. The dynamics of today's economic scenario has made companies to focus their energy and resources on the safety transportation of their goods without any hindrances. Catering to this immediate need, Chartered Logistics Ltd. was established in the year 1995.

Upcoming & Diversified Business

Company use its extensive domain knowledge of transportation business; obtain through working with leading operators in roadways to convert it business into state of art solution. As the dominant professional player in the Indian Transport industry, Chartered Logistics Ltd. reaps the fruits of its superlative technology that surpasses the competition with superior services, spreads across the widest network and has ears on the ground to connect with its loyal and ever expanding customer base.

Market Presense
