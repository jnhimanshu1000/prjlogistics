Why PRJ Logistics?

CROSS FUNCTIONAL DEVELOPMENT
PRJ Provides immense opportunity for cross functional development. Employees are encouraged to take up cross functional roles to groom themselves as good managers and go up in the career ladder. Job Rotation is part of normal career progression which facilitate cross functional movement with higher responsibilities and greater challenges.
LEARNING OPPORTUNITIES
PRJ provides learning opportunities to its employees. All employees undergoes structured training programme. Employees at senior level are sponsored for training. Inhouse training center caters to functional Training needs of employees at junior and middle level.
CAREER FOR LIFE
PRJ offers career for life to employee. One, joining at entry level position can aspire to senior positions through consistent performance. We give opportunities to employees to become part of the growth by learning, & equipping themselves with the changing business dynamics.
LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT
We rely on internal talent development. Adequate attention is paid to leadership development. Employees are assessed against leadership competencies, based on which, future leaders are identified, who are groomed to future leadership positions.

Who are we looking for?

EXPERIENCED PROFESSIONALS
PRJ provides challenging work opportunities to professionals. Solution oriented professionals from across the industry verticals are welcome to explore challenging career opportunities at PRJ.
INTERNSHIP
Students from colleges are engaged to work on live projects on various aspects of supply chain and logistics. This gives opportunity to students to apply their college learning in actual live situation and understand corporate work culture.
